About Me

Hello and welcome to my website!

My name is Silke and this year I started my bachelor’s in Media and Information at the University of Amsterdam.

For a long time I could not wait to go to university, thinking about all the people I would meet and all the memories I would make. Covid-19 hit and all these expectations of living a student-live did not come true.

I started feeling quite lonely and I felt seperated from other students. I feel like I am usually quite the introvert and through the "Media and Information Bibles" (what I call my notes) I felt more confident and connected with the university.

I am a real catperson and I really like trying new things, like making this website for instance! I am also really into Twenty One Pilots, drawing (although I am not that good at it) and movies. Bonding with new people has not been easy the last year so please feel free to contact me about whatever and whenever.
